Quiz
- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
'var' has global scope, can be redeclared, and hoists; 'let' has block scope, cannot be redeclared, and does not hoist.

- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
'var' has global scope, can be redeclared, can be reassigned, and hoists; 'const' has block scope, cannot be redeclared, cannot be reassigned, and does not hoist.

- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
'let' can be reassigned; 'const' cannot.

- What is hoisting?
When a variable is declared with 'var', it is set to undefined at the beginning.
